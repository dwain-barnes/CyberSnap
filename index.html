CyberSnap!

How to Play

1. Click "Deal Card" to reveal a new card.

2. If the new card matches the previous card, quickly click "Snap!"

3. Be faster than the CPU to earn points.

4. The game ends when all cards are dealt.

5. The player with the most points wins!
